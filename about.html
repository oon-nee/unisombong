ABOUT WEB

AboutWeb employees are on the cutting-edge of today’s business world. Their dedicated, customer-centric approach ensures timely and effective results. They come from various educational and cultural backgrounds and have an array of specialties that all contribute to the continued success of AboutWeb.

AboutWeb is committed to employing a diverse workforce. We believe that talented people come from all manner of backgrounds, and our commitment is to find and nurture talented individuals regardless of age, race, color, creed, national origin, sex, gender, or sexual orientation. At AboutWeb, diversity is more than just marketing rhetoric; it is one of our greatest strengths. We believe that diversity of perspective breeds creative, out-of-the-box thinking. A team of ten identical people will all approach a problem the same way. A team of ten individuals who all see the world differently will each have something unique to contribute, allowing for more productive collaboration and more sophisticated problem-solving.

AboutWeb provides a positive work environment. We are focused on providing a healthy work-life balance. To help facilitate this balance, we offer flex time to all employees, we give to neighboring communities, and more.Our objective is to afford every employee with a rewarding career.

AboutWeb is a growing company, and we are always looking for potential leaders not only in technical roles, but in consulting and managerial roles as well. For that reason, our staff is given the tools and environment to excel. AboutWeb is committed to the continued development of its employees.
